Overall Look and Feel of Fastfit
===============================

Our website is very minimalistic and modern. 
The site will be very simple with a clean user interface.

Colours
========
For Fastfit, we wanted the colours of our website to stand out while also having a cool colour tone.
These are our color swatches:

- #0DA1FF
- #0CCCE8
- #00FFD9
- #0CE889
- #0DFF52

Iconography
===========
All of our icons will have a flat design to maintain the minimilistic theme of our website.

Typography
==========
We will be using the font Raleway.

Links
=====
Links will not be underlined and should change color when a user hovers over it.

Buttons
=======
Buttons will be flat designed using the chosen colors outlined above.
