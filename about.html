About

    FastFit ia a company to bring consumer apparel into the 21st century. Our company creates a virtual mannequin of a customer based off detailed body measurements that they provide. From there, users can select from our wide catalogue of apparel and have it displayed to them on their digital body. They will be able to see how well it fits and if they like how the design and colors look on them.
